Cat Finder Example Scenarios

These are example scenarios based on common cat-hiding behavior, not verified customer testimonials.

Behind the laundry machine

After a short treat-bag sound, stop and listen. Use a flashlight to check for eyeshine before moving an appliance.

Inside a closet box

A shy kitten may not answer voices. Try one soft kitten call, pause, and listen for movement in boxes or folded clothes.

Near a recently opened door

Start at the doorway and search along walls, bushes, stairs, and parked cars without chasing.
